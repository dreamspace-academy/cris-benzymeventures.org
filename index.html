About

Cris Silva is a Researcher and Social Entrepreneur. He completed his bachelors degree in biotechnology with honours and is currently a postgraduate student at the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka. He is working in DreamSpace Academy as the guardian of the bio lab and responsible for biotech education, research & development and biotech entrepreneurship. He is the founder of Benzyme Ventures - an initiative focused on the development of bioeconomy, enhancement of research and development output, sustainability, and environmental action through networking, mentoring, and collaboration.
